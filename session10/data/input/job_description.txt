Senior Backend Engineer — FinTech Startup (Series B)
=====================================================

About Us:
We're a fast-growing FinTech company building next-generation payment 
infrastructure. Our platform processes millions of transactions daily
for enterprise clients across Europe and North America.

Role Overview:
We're looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to lead the design and 
development of our core payment processing services. You'll work closely 
with cross-functional teams (product, data, DevOps) to ship reliable, 
high-performance systems at scale.

Must-Have Requirements:
- 5+ years of backend engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python
- Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred)
- Experience designing and building RESTful APIs
- Familiarity with microservices architecture
- Experience with AWS cloud services
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, Jenkins, or similar)
- Strong understanding of software testing practices (unit, integration, e2e)
- Comfortable working in Agile/Scrum teams

Nice-to-Have:
- Experience with Kubernetes or container orchestration
- Familiarity with event-driven architectures (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
- Experience with Terraform or infrastructure-as-code tools
- Exposure to GraphQL
- Experience in the payments/FinTech domain
- Track record of mentoring junior engineers

Responsibilities:
- Lead the design and implementation of backend services for payment processing
- Collaborate with product and data teams to define technical requirements
- Optimize system performance and ensure 99.9% uptime SLAs
- Implement and maintain CI/CD pipelines for automated deployment
- Conduct code reviews and contribute to engineering best practices
- Mentor junior team members and support their growth
- Participate in on-call rotation for critical production systems

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary (€80k–€110k) + equity
- Remote-first culture with quarterly offsites
- Learning budget (€2,000/year)
- Modern tech stack with autonomy to choose the right tools
